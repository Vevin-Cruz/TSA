Fast Sandwich recipe

Ingredients:

  • Burger Buns (or literally any bread)
  • Two slices of deli meat of your choice
  • Two eggs
  • Two slices of cheese of your choice

Recipe:

  1. Whisk your eggs together until combined and season with salt.
  2. Place your pan over medium heat, put the deli meat of choice onto pan and sear for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Once deli meat is cooked remove them and add your eggs and stir to break the forming curd.
  4. Once the egg begins to set place a slice of cheese in the center. Fold the sides of the egg in to form a square that the slice of cheese is in.
  5. Take the cooked egg out and toast your bread with butter.
  6. After you toast the bread assemble your sandwich and place the last slice of cheese in the sandwich, then enjoy.


French Toast

Ingredients:

  • ½ A loaf of bread
  • White sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Black pepper
  • Two eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

Recipe:

  1. Cut your bread into one inch batons.
  2. In a bowl mix together sugar, 2 tsp cinnamon, and ground black pepper.
  3. In a separate bowl mix together eggs, sugar, and 1 and a half tsp of cinnamon until combined, then add milk and mix.
  4. Heat a pan over medium-high heat. Add a slab of butter to the pan while it's heating.
  5. Soak your bread sticks in the egg mixture and add to the hot pan.
  6. Cook all sides for about one minute each or until golden brown.
  7. After the sticks are cooked toss them in the cinnamon sugar and enjoy.


Oat meal

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oats (rolled or quick-cooking)
  • 1 cup liquid (water, milk, or a mix of both)
  • A pinch of salt (optional, enhances flavor)
  • Sweeteners of your choice(honey, maple syrup, or brown sugar)
  • Toppings of your choice(bananas, berries, apples, or dried fruit)

Recipe:

  1. In a small saucepan, mix the oats, liquid, and a pinch of salt (if using).
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, reduce to a simmer, and cook until thickened, you need to stir occasionally
  3. Stir in sweeteners
  4. Mix in your toppings and dig in while it’s warm and creamy.
