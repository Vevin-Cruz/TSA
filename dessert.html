Dessert Recipes

Some quick and easy recipes to satisfy your sweat tooth.

Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter (not chunky)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup lightly packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Recipe:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F. Place a rack in the oven and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with butter and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl mix peanut butter and sugars until combined for 2 minutes, then add egg and baking soda and stir for 2 minutes. Roll into balls and press down with a fork
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes, let cool and enjoy.


Cake Mix Cookies

Ingredients:

  • packet cake mix
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 egg
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Recipe:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F then combine cake mix, vegetable oil, eggs, and chocolate.
  2. Form cookie dough into balls, place them on a baking tray, and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  3. Remove from oven and enjoy.


Almond Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups (285g) granulated sugar
  • 2 egg
  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside
  2. In a large mixing bowl, add your almond flour and baking powder and mix well. Add your melted butter, maple syrup, and vanilla extract, and mix until combined. Using a rubber spatula, fold through your chocolate chips.
  3. Form 6 large or 12 small balls of dough. Place on the lined sheet, and press down into a cookie shape. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes, or until just beginning to go golden around the edges.
  4. Remove from the oven and cool on the baking sheet completely.
